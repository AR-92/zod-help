# Billing and Plans

Everything you need to know about Zod's pricing and billing.

## Pricing

- **Free:** $0/month
- **Pro:** $29/month or $290/year (save 17%)
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing - contact sales

## Usage Limits

### Message Counting

Each message sent to or from an agent counts as one message. This includes:
- User messages to agents
- Agent responses
- System messages
- Tool/plugin calls

### Overages

If you exceed your message limit:
- **Free tier:** Agents pause until next billing cycle
- **Pro tier:** $0.001 per additional message
- **Enterprise:** Custom overage rates

## Managing Your Subscription

### Upgrading

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm the upgrade

Upgrades take effect immediately, and you'll be prorated for the current billing cycle.

### Downgrading

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm the downgrade

Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

### Canceling

You can cancel anytime:
1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Follow the confirmation steps

Your account remains active until the end of the billing period.

## Payment Methods

- PayPal
- Wire Transfer (Enterprise only)

All prices are in USD. Taxes may apply based on your location and will be calculated at checkout.
